1. Analytics Bot Integration in DataPivot Page

An Analytics Bot has been integrated into the DataPivot page to assist users with data exploration, insights, and contextual recommendations.

Users can now interact with the bot by simply clicking on the Ask DataHub button, making it easier to analyze and interpret complex data through natural language queries.
